Walking into a networking event as an introvert takes courage. The noise, the energy, the expectation to be "on" — it can feel like running a marathon before the meeting even starts.

But confidence at networking events isn't about changing who you are. It's about preparation, strategy, and knowing that your quiet presence is an asset, not a liability. Here's how to navigate any networking event with confidence — even if you'd rather be home with a book.
